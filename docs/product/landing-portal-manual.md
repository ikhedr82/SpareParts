# Partivo Landing Portal — User Manual

The Landing Portal is Partivo's public-facing gateway. It serves as the primary marketing, onboarding, and informational hub for potential and existing tenants.

---

## 1. Home Page
**Route**: `/`  
**Purpose**: Present the Partivo value proposition and core features at a glance.

### Sections
- **Hero Section**: High-impact introduction with an immediate Call to Action (CTA) for free signup.
- **Value Proposition**: Explains how Partivo makes spare parts business smarter.
- **Feature Highlights**: Quick cards showing Inventory, Sales, Logistics, and Security.
- **Pricing Preview**: A teaser of the entry-level plan.

---

## 2. Features Detailed
**Route**: `/features`  
**Purpose**: A deep dive into every module available within the SaaS platform.

### Modules Documented
- **Sales & POS**: Transaction recording and cash management.
- **Inventory & Warehouse**: Stock tracking, SKU management, and bin operations.
- **Logistics**: Delivery trip planning and fleet orchestration.
- **Finance**: Invoicing, VAT reporting, and P&L analysis.

---

## 3. Pricing & Plans
**Route**: `/pricing`  
**Purpose**: Transparent breakdown of subscription tiers.

### Plans
- **Free Forever**: For small dealers starting their journey.
- **Professional**: Advanced features for growing businesses.
- **Enterprise**: Custom solutions for high-volume automotive groups.

---

## 4. About Us
**Route**: `/about`  
**Purpose**: Showcasing Partivo's mission and team expertise.

### Fields
- **Mission Statement**: Our goal to modernize the global automotive supply chain.
- **Team Profiles**: Recognition of logistics and engineering experts behind the platform.

---

## 5. Contact & Support
**Route**: `/contact`  
**Purpose**: Direct communication channel for sales and technical inquiries.

### Forms & Actions
- **Get in Touch Form**: Allows users to send direct messages to the Partivo team.
- **Contact Info**: Lists official support emails and headquarters location.

---

## 6. Documentation Shell
**Route**: `/docs`  
**Purpose**: Self-service center for articles, guides, and API documentation.

### Features
- **Global Search**: Find articles quickly.
- **Categories**: Browse via Getting Started, Core Modules, or API Reference.

---

## 7. Authentication Gateway
**Routes**: `/login`, `/signup`  
**Purpose**: Unified entry point for tenant administrators.

### Actions
- **Authorize Access**: Secure login for registered tenants.
- **Create Account**: Seamless onboarding flow with subdomain provisioning.
