# Partivo Product Positioning

## What We Are
Partivo is a vertical, multi-tenant SaaS platform specifically built for the car spare parts retail industry. It functions as a hybrid between a specialized ERP, Shopify, Square POS, and TecDoc.

We offer:
- A shared global spare-parts catalog.
- Branch-level inventory control systems.
- Offline-capable fast POS systems for counter sales.
- Tenant-specific customer-facing digital portals.

## Who We Serve
**Primary Target Market**: Multi-branch car spare parts retailers.
- Businesses that carry thousands of SKUs.
- Businesses selling to workshops (B2B) and end customers (B2C).
- Retailers struggling with stock accuracy and fragmented technical systems.
- Retailers who want a professional digital footprint without building custom IT solutions.

**Who We Do NOT Serve**:
- Wholesalers.
- Parts Manufacturers.
- Horizontal Marketplaces.
- OEM Dealer Networks.

## Competitive Differentiation

| Characteristic | Generic Solutions / Legacy Systems | Partivo Platform |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Architecture** | Custom-built, often on-premise. | Cloud-native, Multi-tenant SaaS. |
| **Setup Process** | Manual, requires massive data entry. | Data-rich day one (preloaded catalogs). |
| **Focus** | Horizontal (General retail or warehousing). | Highly Vertical (Car spare parts exclusively). |
| **The Moat** | Software lock-in. | Catalog and fitment intelligence data. |

Most software in this sector is data-empty out of the box. Partivo's primary differentiator is that retailers do not "enter data"—they subscribe, choose from our existing catalog, map their stock, and start selling immediately.
