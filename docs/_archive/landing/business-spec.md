# Landing Portal Business Specification

## 1. Product Vision
The Partivo SaaS platform aims to provide a robust, multi-tenant solution for businesses. The Landing Portal serves as the primary gateway, designed to convert visitors into active users by clearly communicating value, pricing, and features.

## 2. Target Personas
- **Small Business Owners**: Need a quick, cost-effective digital presence and workflow automation.
- **Enterprise Managers**: Look for scalable, secure, and customizable modules to fit complex organizational structures.
- **Public Users**: Visitors exploring the legal structure (Terms, Privacy) or assessing social proof (Testimonials, FAQs).

## 3. Conversion Funnel Integration
- **Hero Section**: Captures immediate attention with a clear value proposition and primary 'Start Now' Call-To-Action (CTA).
- **Features Section**: Educates the user on specific capabilities mapping to their pain points.
- **Testimonials**: Builds trust through social proof and dynamic user feedback.
- **Pricing & FAQ**: Removes friction and answers common objections prior to conversion.
- **Footer CTA**: A final prompt capturing users who have read the entire page.

## 4. SEO & Localization Strategy
- **Dual Language**: Built from the ground up to support RTL (Arabic) and LTR (English) seamlessly, broadening the TAM (Total Addressable Market) in the MENA region.
- **Dynamic Content Management (CMS)**: Content is decoupled from code, empowering marketing teams to update SEO-critical tags, headlines, and legal text without developer intervention.

## 5. Marketing Copy Guidelines
- Use actionable, benefit-driven language.
- Keep headlines under 8 words.
- Ensure all content mapped to the CMS aligns with the "Production-grade" tone: professional, concise, and trustworthy.
