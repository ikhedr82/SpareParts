Car Spare Parts Retail SaaS Platform

1. Roadmap Philosophy

We do not build everything at once.

We build in three business-driven stages:

Stage 1 – Sell & Onboard
→ Make retailers usable and visible

Stage 2 – Control & Optimize
→ Make operations efficient and profitable

Stage 3 – Monetize & Scale
→ Turn usage into revenue and insights

Every feature must answer:

“Which stage does this serve?”

Goal:
